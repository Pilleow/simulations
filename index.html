Symplectic Euler
Fire Simulation

Inspired by this video. The bottom of the screen heats up the particles. When a particle gets hot, its radius increases, its hexadecimal color value increases, and an upward force is applied relative to the temperature. Heat can be transferred between particles upon collision.

Machine Learning
Evolving Ants

Inspired by this video. Each ant has a genome and a neural net brain. Their genome is a set of instructions on how to connect nodes in the brain. An ant survives if it ends up within the green "leaf" rectangle. Over generations, genomes of surviving ants get passed onto children, with a 1 in 10 000 chance of a mutation.
